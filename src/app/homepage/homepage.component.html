Welcome to PCGuide!

PCGuide is a site meant to share information about Personal Computers (PCs). Computers are an everyday part of our lives, from work to leisure and casual comunication. They are especially important since the COVID-19 pandemic increased opportunities for hybrid and remote work. However, the specifics of getting or building a new computer can be complicated, which is why we're here to help.
