Where do I start with computers?

Getting a new computer can be a complicated process when you don't know what you need! Getting the wrong computer for your needs can be expensive and frustrating.


Besides cost there are a lot of factors to consider. What will you be using it for primarily? What level of power do you want? Do you need to take it on the go? Do you want to be able to modify or upgrade it?
