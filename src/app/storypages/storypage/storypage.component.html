How the Pandemic affected the PC market!

Electronics have become an increasingly regular part of our lives. We carry our phones around in our pockets, cars are becoming increasingly electronically complex, and smarthomes and devices like Alexa are becoming more prominent than ever.


Aside from all this many jobs require computers either for communication or as part of the labor itself. Electronic media and entertainment have become more mainstream with video streaming sites like Netflix, Twitch and Youtube having become increasingly popular over the past decade. Not only that but video games have begun to become one of the dominant forms of entertainment.


So let's look at the history of how COVID-19 exacerbated this focus on technology in our lives.
